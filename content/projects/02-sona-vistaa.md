# Sona Vistaa

At Sona Vistaa in Begur, our team focused on creating a modern, minimalist atmosphere through carefully planned false ceilings and ambient lighting. The subtle glow of the recessed lights highlights the clean lines of the architecture, making the space feel expansive, calm, and incredibly inviting.
